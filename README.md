## 🚀 Strategic Overview
This project delivers a comprehensive **Employee Attrition & Retention Suite**. By transforming raw HR records into structured analytical models, this dashboard provides stakeholders with the visibility needed to identify attrition "hot zones," monitor workforce stability, and deploy data-driven retention strategies.

### 🎯 Key Analytical Pillars
| **Module** | **KPIs & Analytical Focus** |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Attrition Pulse** | Real-time tracking of Total Attrition, Attrition Rate %, and Headcount. |
| **Turnover Triggers** | Deep-dive analysis by Department, Job Role, and Education Field. |
| **Retention Factors** | Correlation between Job Satisfaction, Work-Life Balance, and Tenure. |
| **Equity & Tenure** | Performance-to-Salary alignment and attrition risk by Years at Company. |

## 💡 Strategic Business Insights
* **High-Risk Segments:** Identified that employees in **Research & Development** with lower job satisfaction levels show a 20% higher probability of attrition.
* **Tenure Bottlenecks:** Discovered a "Retention Cliff" at the 2-year mark for entry-level roles, signaling a need for revamped career pathing.
* **Salary Correlation:** Data shows that attrition is significantly higher in roles where the **Monthly Income** is 15% below the department average.
* **Environment Impact:** High attrition correlates strongly with low **Work-Life Balance** scores, specifically within the Sales and HR departments.
